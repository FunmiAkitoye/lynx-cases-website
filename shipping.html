Shipping Policy

At Lynx Cases, we strive to deliver your phone cases quickly and safely. Below is a summary of our shipping procedures:

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped.

Shipping Methods

We use reliable courier services to ensure your order arrives safely. Shipping options may include standard and express delivery, depending on your location.

Delivery Times

Standard shipping usually takes 3-7 business days within Nigeria. Express shipping usually takes 1-3 business days. International shipping times vary by destination.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the chosen delivery method.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your delivery status.

If you have any questions, contact our support team at support@lynxcases.com.
