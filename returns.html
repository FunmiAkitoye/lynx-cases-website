Returns & Refunds Policy

We want you to love your Lynx Cases purchase! If for any reason you are not satisfied, our returns and refunds policy is designed to help.

Eligibility for Returns

Items must be returned within 14 days of receipt. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition you received them.

How to Return

Contact our support team at support@lynxcases.com to initiate a return. Include your order number and reason for return.

Refunds

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Refunds will be applied to your original payment method.

Exchanges

Currently, we only offer refunds. For exchanges, please place a new order and return the previous item.

If you have questions, reach out at support@lynxcases.com.
